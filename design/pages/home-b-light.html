03 Our History

Decades of
visual storytelling

Founded in 1967 by a small group of Chulalongkorn University students with borrowed cameras and a shared obsession with the image, the CU Photo Club has grown into one of Thailand's most respected student photography organisations.

Over five decades, we have documented student life, cultural shifts, and the evolving cityscape of Bangkok — building an archive that is both personal record and historical testimony.

1967

Club Founded

Established by students of the Faculty of Arts

1975

First Public Exhibition

Held at the CU Arts Centre, featuring 40 photographs

1990

Darkroom Established

The club's own darkroom opened on campus

2025

This Website Launches

Sharing our archive with the world for the first time

04 About Us

We see,
therefore

CU Photo Club is a community of students, educators, and photography enthusiasts united by craft and curiosity. We meet weekly, exhibit annually, and document constantly. All skill levels, all disciplines, all welcome.
