For our annual workshop series, members gathered at dawn in Lumphini Park to
study how light transforms the ordinary into something extraordinary. What
followed was not a lesson in technique, but in patience and presence.

We arrived before the sun, cameras cold in our hands, and waited. The first
light came slow — a grey wash, then gold catching the edges of the lake.
